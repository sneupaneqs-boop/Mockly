Our Story

Born from obsession,
refined by craft.

Brūva began in a small roastery in Kathmandu — a single drum roaster, a notebook, and an obsession with precision. We believed that coffee, like any great art form, deserved reverence.

Today we source directly from farmers at altitude, paying above fair-trade premiums. Every gram is roasted to order. No warehousing. No compromise. Just exceptional coffee delivered to your door within 48 hours of roasting.

How We Work

From seed to cup,
nothing is left to chance.

01

Source

Direct relationships with farmers at altitudes above 1,800m. We visit every farm once a year, minimum.

02

Cup

Every lot is cupped 3 times before purchase. We reject over 70% of what we taste.

03

Roast

Small-batch drum roasting on a custom Probat. Every batch logged to the second.

04

Dispatch

Nitrogen-flushed, sealed, and shipped within 48 hours of the roast. Peak flavour, guaranteed.

Where It Comes From

Our Origin Partners

🇪🇹

Ethiopia

Yirgacheffe — 2,100m

The birthplace of coffee. Washed and natural lots from the Gedeo zone, grown under dense forest shade. Floral, tea-like, electric.

🇨🇴

Colombia

Huila — 1,900m

The Andes deliver year-round consistency here. Caramel sweetness, medium body, and a finish that lingers like a good conversation.

🇬🇹

Guatemala

Antigua — 1,700m

Volcanic soil, year-round cool nights. Chocolatey, full-bodied, with a subtle spice that builds on the finish.

🇰🇪

Kenya

Kirinyaga — 1,800m

Double-fermented AA lots from small-holder cooperatives. The most complex coffee on earth. Bold, winey, and utterly alive.
